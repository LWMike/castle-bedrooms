Traditional Bedrooms

Our craftsmen use time-honoured skills to create bespoke traditional bedroom pieces for our clients that they truly love. We pride ourselves in the attention to detail, with each of our fitted furniture pieces adding grandeur and elegance to our traditional bedrooms.

Our traditional fitted furniture is made from equally traditional materials, from the furniture finishes right down to the handles. We will work with you to create a traditional bedroom that you will love, and will be unique to you.

Traditional English Bespoke Carpentry

Over the years we’ve designed and installed beautiful, fully fitted traditional bedroom furniture in many period properties throughout Nottingham and the wider East Midlands.

We can use traditional materials like, Oak, Mahogany, Cherry or very high quality wood look finishes that offer the beautiful appearance of natural wood without the price tag. Our careful use of cornice and pilasters offer an ornate elegance for your home.
