Luxury Bedroom Showroom Keyworth

If you are looking for a luxury bedroom showroom in Keyworth and would like the help of an expert bedroom designer, then look no further than Castle Bedrooms in Keyworth. With over 25 years of experience in designing and installing bespoke bedrooms, we know exactly what it takes to create your perfect bedroom space according to your needs, tastes, and budget too. Whether it’s a master bedroom, children’s room, or bespoke wardrobes you need, we offer a wide choice of styles and finishes ranging from traditional to modern, and all at great value for money.

Over the last 25 years, we have designed and installed many beautiful bedrooms in Keyworth, Nottingham, and the wider area; this includes Derby, Burton, Leicester, Coalville, Ashby De La Zouch, Chesterfield, and Mansfield.

Bespoke Bedroom Furniture

At Castle Bedrooms we know the importance of creating a personalised bedroom space, somewhere to wake up feeling refreshed, and to comfortably retreat to at the end of a long day! The key to achieving this is high-quality bedroom furniture allowing for flexibility and choice within a design. As such all our bedroom furniture is fully bespoke and can accommodate challenging areas such as sloped ceilings, alcoves, chimney breasts or bay windows, fully maximising your space. We’ll even help you plan the internal storage and usage- creating a place for everything so you can keep everything in its place!

Known for their innovative products, as well as precision engineering, our bedroom manufacturers enable us to focus on achieving beautiful, functional, and highly personalised, bedroom designs for the benefit of our clients.
